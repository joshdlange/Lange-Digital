Residential Contracting · Austin, TX
The Problem
Manual workflows eating 15+ hours a week — no automated follow-up, no scheduling system, no visibility into the pipeline. Owner was the bottleneck for everything.
What I Built
An end-to-end operations automation connected to their CRM — automatic follow-up sequences, job scheduling triggers, and a reporting dashboard. Runs without the owner in the loop.
The Result
15 hours a week saved. System paid for itself in the first month. Owner out of the operational loop and back to growing the business.

B2B SaaS · Dallas, TX
The Problem
No repeatable sales process. Every rep handled deals differently. Close rates were inconsistent and declining. CEO didn't know which part of the funnel was breaking.
What I Built
Full sales process from scratch — stage definitions, qualification criteria, discovery framework, objection playbook, CRM setup, and team training. Embedded for 6 months as fractional CRO.
The Result
Best quarter in company history in month four. Team went from winging it to running a consistent, measurable process. Pipeline visibility went from zero to weekly forecasts.

Wellness Studio Group · Chicago, IL
The Problem
Customer support team fielding hundreds of repetitive questions per week — hours wasted on inquiries that could be handled automatically. Staff had no capacity for growth work.
What I Built
A custom AI chatbot trained on their business — hours, policies, services, FAQs. Integrated into their website and booking flow. Escalates edge cases to a human automatically.
The Result
80% of customer inquiries handled automatically. Team freed to focus on growth and retention. Response time near-instant 24/7. Support cost reduced significantly.
